  • Fragged Empire: Tales From the Leyline Road
    A Web Comic based on the Fragged Empire tabletop roleplaying game. Tales From the Leyline Road (TFLR) is a mix of various sci fi genres with inspiration from Borderlands and Guardians of the Galaxy. The setting takes place in the distant future, 10,000 years in the wake of humanity's genetic decline, as various genetically engineered descendants struggle to pick up the pieces of post-war galaxy. Amidst this backdrop of factional disputes, old grudges, and great unknown of a galactic empire abandoned, the crew aboard the Paradigm Shift must learn to work together and evolve beyond the strengths and limitations of their pasts.
